Civil law is a body of rules that defines and protects the private rights of citizens, offers legal remedies that may be sought in a dispute, and covers areas of law such as civil, contracts, tort (for example, throwing a punch in a fight), false imprisonment and defamation, harassment, personal injury, traffic accidents, tenancy obligations, consumer protection, construction and renovation claims, and bankruptcy.
---

Family Proceedings covers cases such as grounds for divorce, nullify of marriage, custody of children, matrimonial assets, Muslim divorce, domestic violence and adoption.
---

Criminal law proscribes conduct perceived as threatening, harmful, or otherwise endangering to the property, health, safety, and moral welfare of people inclusive of one's self. Areas include: Arrest & Investigation, Charge and Bail Bond, Case Management, Pleading Guilty, Mitigation & Sentencing, Trial & Appeal, Magistrate's Complaints and Private Summons
---

Employment law is a broad area encompassing all areas of the employer/employee relationship, consisting of cases with regard to employment rights and work injury compensation act.
---

Technology law provides the legal framework for collecting, storing, and disseminating electronic information in the global marketplace. This concerns (but is not limited to) software licensing, data privacy, cybersecurity, electronic signature and others.
